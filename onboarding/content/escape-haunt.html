Requires ChatGPT Plus

The idea of this GPT is intriguing, and it's implemented very well. The story is exciting and capturing, the art style fits the genre, and the images created are great visual aids to help the players immerse themselves in the story. You try to find your way out of a haunted hotel in much the same fashion as some vintage text-based RPGs like Zork,, which is a playing style with rekindled merit amidst the recent rise in popularity for "Escape Room" games.

The points I have to deduct are not for the game's shortcomings but for ChatGPT itself. As of this writing, the image creation has taken so long that it is almost impossible to stay engaged. When this changes, though, it will be nothing short of a 5-star GPT!
